About me

I was born in Mogi das Cruzes and, at the age of 14, I moved to São José dos Campos to support my brother, who would be the first in the family to attend college. There, I finished high school at the state school Francisco Lopes de Azevedo, where I was able to explore my interests and begin my academic and professional journey.

During high school, I took part in volunteer projects aimed at democratizing education in Brazil, focusing on science olympiads. As the director and deputy coordinator of the Media and Design department, I led impactful initiatives, honing valuable communication and design skills. These experiences ultimately earned me a full scholarship to study Computer Science at INSPER in São Paulo.
